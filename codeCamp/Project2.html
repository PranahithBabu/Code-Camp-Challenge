Maggi Recipe

Maggi is the only food which I actually know to do independently with no help. This food actually gives me some boost to live independently, just kidding!!! 😜

Ingredients

  • 300ml of water.
  • 1 small Maggi packet.
  • 1 carrot 🥕 & 1 potato 🥔(not mandatory).

Procedure

  1. Take bowl & pour the water, then boil it in high steam.
  2. During the time of boiling add the masala.
  3. After 2-3 min of boiling, add the maggi blocks.
  4. Then add carrots & potatoes if cut into small pieces.
  5. Then boil it in a low steam for 4-5 min.
  6. Keep stirring it & finally take it into a bowl.

Enjoy your breakfast!!! 🍜
